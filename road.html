Why Choose Us

Our Capabilities:

  • Spray & Chip Surfacing — cost-effective sealing for rural and feeder roads
  • Hot & Cold Asphalt — for flexible pavements with enhanced durability
  • Rigid Pavements — concrete surfacing for high-load, industrial zones
  • Road Rehabilitation — resurfacing, patching, and strengthening of worn roads
  • Drainage Works — plastering, painting, tiling, and final fit-outs

Materials and Methods

We use certified bitumen binders, asphalt mixes, and aggregates produced from our in-house plants. Our surfacing crews are trained for speed, accuracy, and safety across every job site.

Results That Last

From mine roads to commercial estates, MANDFAR-built surfaces are known for their finish quality, water resistance, and long service life under heavy use.
