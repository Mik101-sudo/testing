Asphalt Plant

MANDFAR operates a dedicated asphalt plant with the capacity to produce 150 tons of high-quality mix daily. By controlling our own asphalt supply, we reduce project lead times, cut material costs, and ensure consistent surface quality across all our road and civil infrastructure projects.
